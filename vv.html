Microsoft Office Package

A comprehensive course covering the full Microsoft Office suite — essential for academics, internships, and professional life.

  • ▸MS Word — Document creation, formatting, report writing, and professional letters
  • ▸MS Excel — Spreadsheets, formulas, data entry, charts, and basic data analysis
  • ▸MS PowerPoint — Slide design, presentation skills, and visual storytelling
  • ▸MS Outlook & Teams — Email management, scheduling, and digital collaboration tools

Learn Canva from beginner to advanced — create stunning visuals for social media, academics, events, and professional branding.

  • ▸Presentations & Posters — Eye-catching slides, event posters, and infographics
  • ▸Branding & Logos — Create personal brand kits, logos, and business card designs
  • ▸Portfolio Projects — Build a real design portfolio to showcase skills to colleges and employers
